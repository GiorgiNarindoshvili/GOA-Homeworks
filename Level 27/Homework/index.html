Japanese Cars

Acura TLX-The Acura TLX is an impressive four-door entry-level luxury sedan that serves as a successor to both the TL and TSX models. Combining performance, luxury, and cutting-edge technology, this remarkable vehicle stands as the flagship sedan in Acura's lineup following the discontinuation of the RLX. Under the hood, the TLX is equipped with a powerful 3.0-liter V-6 engine, providing ample power to satisfy any need for speed while maintaining a smooth and enjoyable drive. Notably, its standout feature is the Super Handling All-Wheel Drive system, intelligently distributing torque between all four wheels to enhance cornering abilities and deliver impressive road grip. Additionally, the Precision All-Wheel Steer system further adds to the car's agility by adjusting the rear wheel angles during turns.

Toyota Supra-Coming in at No. 1 is the world’s first production model of the 2020 Toyota Supra GR signed by Toyota chief executive Akio Toyoda. It sold for a whopping $2.1 million at Barrett-Jackson’s annual auction in Scottsdale, Arizona in 2019. Note, however, that the car was sold to benefit a charity; the entire bid amount went to the American Heart Association and the Bob Woodruff Foundation. Nonetheless, it was a thrilling moment at Barrett-Jackson as bids came in at $100,000 increments faster than a blink of an eye until reaching the final bid of $2.1 million. Aside from it being the first production model of 2020, it was painted in matte-gray over a red interior with red mirror caps and matte-black wheels, making it a true one of a kind.

Lexus RC-The RC model range was first unveiled at the 2013 Tokyo Motor Show, and among its offerings is the Lexus RC F, a sleek and compact executive two-door coupé. The RC F boasts an impressive performance, thanks to its 472 horsepower 5.0-liter V-8 engine. The engine paired with an adaptive suspension, upgraded Brembo brakes, and a limited-slip rear differential provides excellent power and handling capabilities. Power is channeled to the rear wheels through an eight-speed automatic transmission.Design-wise, the RC F looks like it's been chiseled by a futuristic sculptor who's also a bit of a rebel. The interior is no slouch either, getting into the seats is like getting a big, comfortable hug from a posh cloud. The designers really went all out with top-notch leather-trimmed seats and plush surfaces that make you feel like you're floating on a magic carpet of opulence.
